chicken alfredo recipe

home page

Ingredients

Fettuccine sauce

Chicken breast

herbs and spices

butter

garlic

heavy cream

parmesan cheese

Steps

Cook chicken in a large saute pan for about 4-7 minutes per side

Cut butter into 8 slices, add to large skillet, and reduce heat to low. add garlic and cook for 30-60 seconds, stir frequently.

Whisk in heavy cream

Remove the pan from heat and stir in Parmesan cheese, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.

Add cooked pasta and toss to coat evenly. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Add chicken and return to medium-low heat for a couple minutes or until everything is warmed through
